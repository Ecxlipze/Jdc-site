Access to Tools That Enable Learning and Care

In many communities, learning and basic care are limited not by ability or effort, but by the lack of simple tools. The Madad Digital Access & Tools Program exists to help bridge this gap by making essential digital tools available where they are needed most.

This program treats technology as a support, something that helps people do their work better, not as a solution on its own.

What This Program Supports

The program provides practical, carefully chosen digital support that fits real conditions, including:

  • Shared or institution-based digital devices that can be used by many
  • Offline and low bandwidth learning resources for areas with limited connectivity
  • Basic digital tools that support health and education programs
  • Technology selected to match local needs, skills, and infrastructure

All tools are chosen for their simplicity, durability, and usefulness ‚Äî not for their novelty.

How Support Is Delivered

Support under this program is delivered through:

  • Schools and learning centers
  • Clinics and community institutions
  • Structured deployment plans that align with existing Madad Farma programs

Madad Farma avoids direct device distribution to individuals. Shared access is prioritized where it is more sustainable and fairer ensuring tools serve communities rather than individuals alone.
